About Us





Who are we?



E-Supply started off as a supplier of wire harnesses. In order to better serve our clients' needs, the company has now broadened its product line to encompass a variety of electronic components, including cables wires, IC chips, semiconductors, and more.

E-Supply has been in business for 13 years and now provides services to more than 500 clients in Asia. To provide effective support for all customer questions and concerns, we have set up warehouses and sales offices in strategic locations such as Thailand, China, Malaysia, Indonesia, Vietnam, Japan, and Singapore.
